Reading Plus SeeReader Hack

Updated April 5, 2018. This tool allows you to save the story you're reading so you can get back to it while answering the questions.

  2. When you're on a SeeReader passage, there will be a variety of sections to it (usually 3-5 sections). You can view how many exist by PAUSING the passage and looking at the box in the bottom center.

  3. For each section, CLICK the bookmark ONCE. Below the passage should appear a block of text with the title. It should look like this:

  4. Every time you reach a new section, click the bookmark. Repeat until the story is over.
